The Challenge Facing Kerala's Farmers

  • Generic Agricultural Advice
    Issue: Farmers often receive one-size-fits-all guidance that doesn’t consider local crops, soil, or weather conditions
    Impact: Leads to wasted resources, inefficient practices, and lower productivity
  • Lack of Personalized Support
    Issue: No context-aware advice tailored to specific farm conditions or farming methods
    Impact: Farmers find it hard to optimize yields and improve their techniques
  • Poor Record Keeping
    Issue: Farmers struggle to maintain accurate logs of activities, crop cycles, and expenditures
    Impact: Limits learning from past experience and access to government schemes requiring documentation
  • Knowledge Gap
    Issue: Limited access to timely, actionable agricultural information during critical crop growth stages
    Impact: Informed decision-making is difficult, leading to lower crop success rates

Core Features of Krishi Sakhi

📋

Farmer & Farm Profiling

Comprehensive capture of key details of farmer including Name, Email, Gender, DOB and of Farm location, Land size, Crop types, Soil type, Season and Water resources available for personalized recommendations.

📅

Smart Event Calendar

Schedule key farm activities like sowing, irrigation, and harvesting. The app generates automated reminders and countdowns for each stage of the crop cycle, ensuring farmers never miss a critical task.

🌦️

Real-Time Weather Alerts

Get instant weather updates based on your farm’s location. Receive alerts like “Don’t sow today, rain is coming!” to save seeds, money, and effort.

🤖

Intelligent Chatbot Assistance

Interact through text, voice, or crop images. The AI chatbot responds instantly in both text and audio, making it easy even for farmers with low literacy.

📈

Crop Price Analytics

Check market prices for crops by entering crop name, city, and state. View detailed graphs showing minimum, maximum, and average prices to make smarter selling decisions.

⏰

Personalized Reminders & Notifications

Receive proactive alerts for farm tasks, weather changes, and upcoming crop events. Stay informed and reduce crop loss with timely guidance.
